# Reordering & Reparenting

Parts, chapters, and scenes can be rearranged by dragging them in the [navigation tree](#help:manuscript.nav-tree).

- Drag a **scene** to a new position, or into a different chapter.
- Drag a **chapter** to reorder it, move it into or out of a part.
- Drag a **part** to reorder it within the book.

Chapter and part numbers are computed automatically from their position, so moving things renumbers everything for you. Blank-titled items simply display as *Chapter N* or *Part I*.
