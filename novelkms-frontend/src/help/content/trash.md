# Trash & Soft Delete

Deleting a project, book, chapter, scene, or Codex item does **not** destroy it. It is soft-deleted and moved to **Trash**, where it can be restored.

- **Restore** returns an item to the end of its original parent.
- **Purge** permanently removes a single item.
- **Empty Trash** permanently removes everything in Trash.

Only purge and empty-trash are irreversible. Parts are an exception: deleting a part promotes its chapters to the book and is not sent to Trash.
