# Click-to-Scroll

When a review finding includes a short verbatim quote from your text ("anchor text"), you can click the finding's location to scroll to and briefly highlight that passage in the editor.

- The highlight is temporary and is never saved into your manuscript.
- Older reviews created before this feature may not have anchor text, so their findings are not clickable.
