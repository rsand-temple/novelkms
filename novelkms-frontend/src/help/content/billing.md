# Subscription & Billing

Manage your subscription under **Settings → Billing**.

- **Subscribe** opens secure Stripe-hosted checkout.
- **Manage billing** opens the Stripe customer portal to update payment methods, view invoices, or cancel.

If a subscription is required and yours is inactive, NovelKMS routes you here automatically. A scheduled cancellation keeps your access until the end of the current period.

Bringing your own AI key is separate from your NovelKMS subscription — see [AI credentials](#help:ai.credentials).
