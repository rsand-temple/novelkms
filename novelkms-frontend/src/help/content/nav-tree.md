# The Navigation Tree

The left panel shows your whole project as a tree: **Project → Book → Part → Chapter → Scene**, with the [Codex](#help:codex.overview) and AI document leaves at the bottom of each book and chapter.

- Click any node to open it in the editor.
- Click a **chapter** to edit all its scenes together; click a single **scene** to edit just that one.
- Right-click any node for its [context menu](#help:manuscript.context-menu) (rename, add, delete, AI actions, and more).

Selecting a node also drives the Properties panel and determines the scope of [search](#help:editor.search) and [export](#help:import-export.export).

See also: [drag and drop](#help:manuscript.drag-drop), [Trash](#help:manuscript.trash).
