# The Navigation Tree

The left panel shows your whole project as a tree. The full hierarchy is:

**Project → Book → Part → Chapter → Scene**

Below the scenes in each chapter are three fixed AI document leaves: **Memory**, **Summary**, and **Editorial**. Below each book is a **Summary** leaf and a **Codex** section. At the bottom of each project is an **Artifacts** section.

## Selecting nodes

- Click any node to open it in the center panel.
- Clicking a **scene** edits just that scene. Clicking a **chapter**, **part**, or **book** edits all its scenes together while still saving each scene independently.
- Clicking a **Memory**, **Summary**, or **Editorial** leaf opens that AI document in the editor — the same editor, just in a different mode.
- Clicking a folder in **Artifacts** opens the Artifacts Explorer in the center panel.

## Other navigation actions

- Right-click any node for its [context menu](#help:manuscript.context-menu) (rename, add, delete, AI actions, and more).
- Press **F2** on a selected node to rename it inline.

Your navigation selection also drives the Properties panel and determines the scope of [search](#help:editor.search) and [export](#help:import-export.export).
