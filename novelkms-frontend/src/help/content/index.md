# Welcome to NovelKMS

NovelKMS is a novel knowledge-management system with a manuscript editor attached. It helps you draft long-form fiction while keeping the surrounding project knowledge — structure, Codex, review history, and exports — in one durable place.

This is the help center. Browse the sections on the left, search at the top, or click any **?** button next to a control to jump straight to the relevant topic.

## The big picture

Your work is organized as a hierarchy:

- **Project** — the top container.
- **Book** — a manuscript within a project.
- **Part** — an optional grouping of chapters.
- **Chapter** — holds one or more scenes.
- **Scene** — the unit that actually gets saved, even when you edit a whole chapter at once.

Start with [navigating the manuscript](#help:manuscript.nav-tree), learn the [editor](#help:editor.overview), then explore [AI review](#help:ai.overview) when you are ready for editorial feedback.

## Where things live

- The **top bar** holds Export, Import, Templates, Settings, and this Help center.
- The **left panel** is your manuscript navigation tree.
- The **center** is the editor.
- The **right panel** shows properties and the AI review rail.
