# Your First Book

A quick path from empty workspace to writing.

1. Create a **Project** to hold your work.
2. Add a **Book** inside it.
3. Add a **Chapter** — NovelKMS automatically creates a first scene for you.
4. Click the scene and start writing in the [editor](#help:editor.overview).

You can reorder and reparent chapters and scenes at any time by [dragging them in the navigation tree](#help:manuscript.drag-drop). Nothing is ever truly lost — deletions go to [Trash](#help:manuscript.trash) first.

When a draft is far enough along, try an [AI review](#help:ai.overview) of a chapter, or [export](#help:import-export.export) to Word.
