# Search & Replace

Find and replace text across the scope you have selected — the current scene, chapter, part, or book.

- Matches are highlighted in place as transient decorations; they are never saved into your manuscript.
- Navigating between matches does not change your manuscript selection.

The scope follows your [navigation tree](#help:manuscript.nav-tree) selection, so select a book to search the whole book, or a single scene to stay narrow.
