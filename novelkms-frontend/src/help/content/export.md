# Exporting

Use the **Export** menu in the top bar. What you can export depends on your [navigation tree](#help:manuscript.nav-tree) selection:

- **Book** as Word (.docx) or ePub.
- **Part**, **Chapter**, or **Scene** as Word (.docx), when one is selected.
- **Project archive** (.json) — a portable backup of the whole project.

Exports build from your stored hierarchy and templates, preserving scene boundaries rather than flattening everything into one blob. Portability is a core goal: your work is never locked in.
