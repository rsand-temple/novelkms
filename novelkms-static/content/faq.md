## Your manuscript and data

### Who owns my manuscript?

You do. NovelKMS is a workspace for organizing and revising your manuscript. It does not claim ownership of your writing.

### Can I export my work?

Yes. NovelKMS is built around portability. DOCX import and export are part of the workflow, so your manuscript is not trapped inside the app.

### What happens if I cancel?

You can export your manuscript and project materials in one single operation before leaving the service. We are committed to making leaving as easy as joining, without losing control of your work.

## AI features

### Does NovelKMS write my book for me?

No. NovelKMS uses AI for review, summaries, memory documents, and organization. It does not write or rewrite your manuscript or ever directly touch the content of your book.

### What does bring your own AI key mean?

AI features use an API key that you provide in your NovelKMS settings. This keeps AI usage under your control instead of bundling hidden AI credits into the subscription. We support OpenAI, Gemini, and Anthropic.

Can I use NovelKMS without AI?

Absolutely! The manuscript editor, project structure, Codex, import, export, and organization features are all useful regardless of AI review.

## Billing

### Is there a free trial?

All users are granted a 14-day trial.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Billing is managed through Stripe. You can manage or cancel your subscription from the Billing tab in NovelKMS settings.

### What payment processor does NovelKMS use?

NovelKMS uses Stripe for checkout, subscriptions, invoices, payment methods, and cancellation management. You will never credit card information on our site.

## Product maturity

### Is NovelKMS finished?

I hope not! NovelKMS is actively developed. The core manuscript, AI review, summaries, Codex, authentication, billing, and administration features are in place. The product will continue to evolve based on user feedback, including my own.

### Who is NovelKMS best for right now?

NovelKMS is best for serious long-form fiction authors who want manuscript structure, story knowledge, summaries, and AI-assisted review in one workspace.

## Support

### How do I report a bug or ask for help?

Use the \'Contact Support\' page provided in the app, or e-mail us anytime at support@novelkms.com.

### Can I request features?

Definitely! Subscriber feedback will strongly influence ongoing development priorities.
