## Bring your own AI key

NovelKMS uses a bring-your-own-key model for AI providers. That means AI usage is billed by your AI provider account, not hidden inside NovelKMS pricing.

## Subscription access

NovelKMS is designed to support subscription access through Stripe, including checkout, billing management, and cancellation through Stripe-hosted pages.

## Early access

During early access, subscription enforcement and access policies may change as the product matures.
