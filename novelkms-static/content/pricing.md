## Founders access

NovelKMS is currently in early access. Start a 14-day trial with no credit card required, then continue as an early subscriber for a flat $9/month.

## Bring your own AI key

NovelKMS uses a bring-your-own-key model for AI providers: OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini. Your AI usage is billed directly by your own provider account. NovelKMS charges a flat subscription fee and never marks up or resells AI usage.

## Subscription access

Subscriptions are managed through Stripe: checkout, billing management, and cancellation all happen on Stripe-hosted pages. You can cancel anytime from the Billing tab in NovelKMS settings.

## Early access

During early access, subscription enforcement and access policies may change as the product matures.
