NovelKMS uses AI to help authors review, summarize, and organize revision work. It does not automatically rewrite your manuscript.

## Review, not replacement

The author remains in control. AI review can surface concerns, inconsistencies, continuity questions, or revision opportunities, but NovelKMS does not modify manuscript text on its own.

## Findings you can triage

AI recommendations are stored as persistent review artifacts. You can mark findings done, dismiss them, defer them, reopen them, or promote useful material into the Codex.

## Memory, summaries, and editorials

NovelKMS supports several AI-assisted document types:

- Memory documents for continuity context.
- Chapter summaries and book summaries.
- Editorial readings for broader tone, arc, and story observations.

These are project artifacts, not chat transcripts.
